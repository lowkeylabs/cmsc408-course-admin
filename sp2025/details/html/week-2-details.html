Week 2 - Details

2025-01-21

How do you craft a database? How do you represent the real world in a database?

The lectures this week will focus on designing databases and on the basics of entity-relationship modeling. We’ll be doing lots of examples to get practice.

Things to do this week

  1. Deliverable 1 - Intros - If you haven’t already, complete project deliverable 1. In this assignment you’re introducint yourself Harmonize and meeting others in the class. One goal is to find a partner for the semester-long project.
  2. Deliverable 2 - Team formation - Once you find a partner, complete this on-line survey to tell me about your team.
  3. Practice Quiz 1 - Start Practice Quiz 1. You can take this quiz as many times as you like. The real quiz is scheduled for next week, Week 3. If you’re comfy, you can take it as soon as it opens, no need to wait.
  4. Homework 1 - Data Engineering Tool Review - this is homework assignment is due Sunday.
    Don’t wait to the last minute. There is lots to do and tools to install.
  5. Discussion 1 - ER Modeling - Practice, practice, practice. This Harmonize discussion allows you to model a database using words, and get feedback from others in class. The discussions asks that you turn the tables, offering feedback to others on their database model.
