2025-01-10

The class hasn’t yet started, but here are some things you can be doing to keep ahead of the curve.

Things to do this week!

  1. Introduce yourself - The course will require completion of a team-based semester-long project. Your goal during the first week of the course is to find a partner. It starts with introductions.

  2. Take the pre-class survey - I’d like to learn more about the students in my class. Besides the introduce yourself stuff from above, I’ve got a survey with a bunch of questions. The survey is graded. That means that you get credit for completing it.

  3. Join the course discord - Previous classes appreciated a place to get their questions answered by others in the same class.

  4. Watch lecture 1 - Of course you’ll need to wait until it’s released. I’ll be covering lots of info about how the class works, grading, and other course mechanics. We’ll also start on databases!

  5. Review the assignments. It will be a busy semester and things will jump at you quickly! Here is a gantt chart of the deliverables so that you can see how they all fit together.

About this page

This page represents the frontpage for every week. The frontpage lists the deliverables due this week, provides links to the lecture slides and video (after it’s recorded), and any helpful resources and references.

The frontpage presents a set of quick links to any week in the semester, and also highlights the current week. You can always press Home on the top left of the Canvas window to return to the current week’s frontpage.
