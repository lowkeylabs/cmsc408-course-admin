We’re plowing through all the normal form concepts, vocabulary, algorithms and examples. It’s a lot.

How will it help? Understanding normal forms can help you design efficient and accurate databases.

Will you ever need to normalize a database? Heck yeah! If you do any data science at all, you’ll often receive dirty and denormal data. Data cleaning is all about cleaning the data and then … wait for it … NORMALIZING it so that you can be assured it’s even cleaner!
